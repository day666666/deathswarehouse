What is Death's Warehouse?

Death’s warehouse is a concept created from a dream I had where my siblings and I died in a car accident. I wanted to capture the feeling of being somewhere dreamlike, borderline nightmarish, but also soothing and nostalgic in a way… The main three characters are very loosely based on my siblings, but they more reflect parts of myself.
